## About Me

I'm **Vrunda Bramhe**, a B.Tech student in **Artificial Intelligence** at St. Vincent Pallotti College of Engineering, Nagpur (CGPA: 9.68), graduating in 2027. I enjoy building things that sit at the intersection of **web development, data analytics, and automation** — from full-stack apps to Power BI dashboards that turn raw data into decisions.

### What Drives Me

I like taking a problem apart and rebuilding it as something usable — whether that's a platform that helps farmers rent out idle equipment, a script that automates repetitive job applications, or a dashboard that makes sales data easy to read at a glance. I'm currently deepening my skills in AI/ML and data analytics while staying hands-on with frontend and backend development.
